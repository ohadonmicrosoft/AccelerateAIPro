## Animation Specifications

### Timing Constants
- Primary: 360ms
- Secondary: 300ms
- Micro: 200ms
- Page: 400ms

### Motion Properties
- Easing: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0.0, 0.2, 1)
- Scale: 0.98 - 1.02
- Opacity: 0.6 - 1
- Translation: 2px - 4px
